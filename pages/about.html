About me

Hi, I’m Anargya, a first-year medical student at Universitas Muhammadiyah Yogyakarta (UMY), Class of 2025. I’m passionate about medicine, education, and creative storytelling — which is why I create content that shares the real journey of becoming a doctor.

As a medical content creator, I aim to make medical topics more approachable and help others navigate the challenges of med school — from study tips and academic resources to mental health and motivation. I believe learning should be engaging, relatable, and meaningful.

Whether you're a fellow med student, an aspiring doctor, or someone interested in the world of healthcare, I hope my content can inform, inspire, and support you along the way.

On My Journey

  • Medical Student

    Sharing the real experience of being a med student — from anatomy labs and OSCEs to late-night study sessions and exam stress. It’s not just about studying medicine, but about growing through it.

  • Tech Enthusiast

    I love exploring digital tools that make life as a medical student more efficient — whether it’s productivity apps, smart note-taking systems, or tech that helps me study smarter, not harder.

  • Mobile Photography

    Capturing everyday moments with just a phone — from campus life and study cafés to golden hour skies after class. Because there’s beauty in the small, unfiltered pieces of life.

  • Videography

    Combining visuals and narrative to create content that informs, inspires, and resonates. I use storytelling to make medicine more human, and videography as a tool to bring that story to life.
