About mee

Hi, I’m Anargya, a first-year medical student at Universitas Muhammadiyah Yogyakarta (UMY), Class of 2025. I’m passionate about medicine, education, and creative storytelling — which is why I create content that shares the real journey of becoming a doctor.

As a medical content creator, I aim to make medical topics more approachable and help others navigate the challenges of med school — from study tips and academic resources to mental health and motivation. I believe learning should be engaging, relatable, and meaningful.

Whether you're a fellow med student, an aspiring doctor, or someone interested in the world of healthcare, I hope my content can inform, inspire, and support you along the way.
